| `concepts/carbon.md` | Scope 1/2/3 emissions, carbon intensity, grid carbon factor, LL97 |
| `concepts/benchmarks.md` | ASHRAE, CBECS, ENERGY STAR — what good looks like by building type and climate zone |

Use `_templates/concept.md` for each file.
